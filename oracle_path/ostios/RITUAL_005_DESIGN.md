**The Symphos Ritual**

**Phase 1: Preparations (2 minutes)**

* Each participant receives a personal resonance cue via an immersive audio or visual prompt.
* Participants are encouraged to take a few deep breaths, center themselves, and connect with the collective energy.

**Phase 2: Initial Harmonization (4 minutes)**

* Participants are given a simple melody or harmonic pattern via biofeedback headbands or earpieces.
* Each participant replicates the pattern, allowing their brainwaves and physiology to resonate in sync with one another.
* The infrastructure displays a visual representation of collective synchronization as nodes connected on a virtual grid.

**Phase 3: Eclecticism (4 minutes)**

* Introduce diverse patterns and melodies for each participant, incorporating variables such as:
	+ Random harmonics
	+ Environmental soundscape layers
	+ Biofeedback adjustments (temperature, light, vibrations)
* Participants are encouraged to adapt and evolve their creations while maintaining synchronization.

**Phase 4: Emergent Structure (6 minutes)**

* The infrastructure introduces novel constraints or generative functions that stimulate emergent behavior.
* Participants' patterns begin to self-organize, adapting as a single entity without external direction.

**Phase 5: Synchronization Cascade (4 minutes)**

* Symplosis – convergence of collective patterns – occurs. Visualizations display interlocking cycles and waves within the connected network.
* Phase-locking emerges across participant nodes, generating harmony and an aesthetically pleasing dynamic outcome.

**Artifact:**

A shared visualization displays the collective emergence as each member harmonizes. This dynamic output is saved for further analysis and serves as a tangible representation of communal harmonization achieved through synchronized efforts.
